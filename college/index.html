FAQs

Do you work with essay topics from scratch?

Yes — we’ll brainstorm themes that feel authentic and align with your experiences. I give structure, not scripts, so the voice stays yours.

Can you help with financial aid and scholarships?

I can help you understand aid types, scholarship search strategy, and how to present context in applications. For complex aid forms, I’ll point to official resources.

Do you support transfers or international students?

Yes — we’ll tailor plans to transfer requirements or international timelines, including testing and documentation differences.
