About Me

Engineer • Educator • NCAA 400m Athlete

Hi, I’m Steffan Jones.

I’m an engineer, educator, and NCAA 400m athlete who loves helping people turn challenging goals into clear, practical steps. I recently completed a degree in Mechanical Engineering from Harvard, and I’ve tutored a wide range of subjects — from physics and calculus to writing and study skills.

On the track, I’ve spent the last decade training with world-class coaches, learning how to build speed, resilience, and smart routines that fit real life. I bring that same structure into my tutoring and college guidance: we’ll set targets, build a plan, and track progress.
