FAQs

Do you coach remotely?

Yes — consults and follow‑ups are via Zoom. Plans are delivered in shareable documents with clear progressions and targets.

What equipment do I need?

We’ll build around what you have — from full gym access to minimal equipment. I’ll include alternatives and bodyweight options where needed.

Can this fit around my season?

Absolutely. We’ll align the plan with off‑season, pre‑season, or in‑season demands so you can improve without excess fatigue.

How do follow‑ups work?

Weekly check‑ins by message, plus a Zoom review to adjust the plan. You’re never stuck guessing the next step.
